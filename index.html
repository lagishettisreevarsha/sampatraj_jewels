Our Legacy

A Trusted Legacy of Purity and Craftsmanship in Secunderabad since 1984

1984

The Founding Roots

Our journey began in Pot Market, Secunderabad, with a simple promise: to provide pure traditional jewellery with transparent, honest pricing. Founded as a family boutique, we quickly gained the community's trust.

1998

Diamond & Pearl Expansion

Embracing the rich Nizami heritage of Hyderabad, we expanded our curation to fine uncut diamond (Polki) sets and selected Basra and South Sea pearls, catering to royal bridal designs.

2012

100% Purity Certification

We became one of the early adopters in the region to fully guarantee BIS Hallmarked gold and certified diamonds. Our commitment to transparent pricing and verified purity sets us apart.

Today

Modern Traditionalists

Decades later, we continue to serve multiple generations of families. While design methods modernize, our values of integrity, quality craft, and personal care remain unchanged.

Trusted Family Business

Managed directly by family craftsmen, ensuring personal care, attention, and lifelong relationships.

Generations of Craft

Our experienced karigars combine traditional techniques with state-of-the-art detail.

Purity Guaranteed

Every piece is certified with BIS Hallmarking for Gold and accredited laboratory certification for Diamonds.

Transparent Pricing

No hidden costs. Complete breakup of gold weight, stones, diamonds, and making charges detailed upfront.
